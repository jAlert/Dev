# General Process Flow for Policy Proposals Module

## Initial Review of Documents

Step 1. User: Proponent

	- Create and Submit Proposal

Step 2. User: TRC Secretariat

	- Review submitted documents. If the documents are complete, forward it to the reviewers and if not return it to the proponent

Step 3. User: Reviewer

	- Provide comments. after 10days deadline or no comment this will automatically forwarded to TRC Secretariat for deliberation. If there are comments this will return to proponent for revision of the proposal

Step 3.1 User: Proponent

	- Revise the proposal and when done, forward it to the TRC Secretariat for deliberation

## For TRC Deliberation

Step 4. User: TRC Secretariat
	
    - After the deliberation, upload minutes of meeting and forward it for PTWG for endorsement; but if there's revision needed this will return to proponent for revision of the proposal. TRC secretariat will decide whether this will be for deliberation again or ad referendum.

Step 4.1 User: Proponent (ad refendum path)
	
    - Revise the proposal and when done, forward it to the TRC Secretariat for ad referendum. 10days deadline.

Step 4.1.1 User: Reviewer 
	
    - Provide comments. after 10days deadline or no comment this will automatically forwarded to TRC Secretariat for PTWG endorsement.

Step 4.1.2 User: TRC Secretariat
	
    - TRC Secretariat will endorse the proposal to PTWG. If there are comments this will return to proponent for revision of the proposal. Secretariat will decide whether this will be for deliberation again or ad referendum.

Step 4.1.3 User: Proponent
	
    - Revise the proposal and when done, forward it to the TRC Secretariat.

Step 4.1.4 User: TRC Secretariat
	
    - TRC Secretariat will choose whether this is for TRC deliberation or ad referendum. this will loop until no comments from the reviewers. If no comments from the reviewers, this will be forwarded to PTWG for endorsement.

Step 4.2 User: Proponent (TRC deliberation path)
	
    - Revise the proposal and when done, forward it to the TRC Secretariat for deliberation. 10 days deadline.

Step 4.2.1 User: TRC Secretariat

    - After the deliberation, upload minutes of meeting and decide whether this will be for deliberation again or ad referendum.

Step 4.2.2 User: Proponent (ad referendum path)

    - go to step 4.1

Step 4.2.3 User: Proponent (TRC deliberation path)

    - go to step 4.2

## For PTWG Endorsement and Approval

Step 5. User: TRC Secretariat (PTWG manual process)

    - TRC Secretariat will wait for the PTWG meeting to discuss the proposal. If there are comments secretariat will upload the minutes of meeting and this will return to proponent for revision of the proposal. Secretariat will forward it to TRC for deliberation. back to step 4.2. if no comments this will proceed to manual proccess of signing and publication of the policy then forward back to TRC Secretariat to upload it.
